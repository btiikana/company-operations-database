# Decision Log

Project: Island Supply Company Operations Database System

Purpose: Record important project decisions and explain why they were made.

## 2026-06-19 - Use Piece-Based Course Filtering

**Decision:**
I will not complete courses blindly from start to finish. I will study only the course topics that support the current project piece and artifact.

**Reason:**
The goal is to build a complete database engineering project, not just collect course notes. Each topic should connect to a real artifact in the Island Supply Company system.

**Impact:**
This keeps the learning path focused and prevents wasted study time.

---

## 2026-06-19 - Focus on Business Analysis Before Database Design

**Decision:**
Piece 0 will focus on business process and requirements thinking before any tables, ERDs, or SQL files are created.

**Reason:**
A database should be designed from real business needs, stakeholders, workflows, problems, and requirements.

**Impact:**
The first major artifact will be `docs/business_process_map.md`.
